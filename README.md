The GameFair License (GF-V1) is a system of licensing designed for creators of mods, fangames, and fanfictions. It aims to protect the fan community from commercial exploitation while promoting ethical and transparent sharing.

## THE OBLIGATORY ETHICAL CORE (GF BASE RULES)

**ALL GameFair variations (SA, ND, AU, CC) MUST comply with the following rules, without exception:**

| Core Rule | Impact on the Fan Creator |
| :--- | :--- |
| üõ°Ô∏è **Mandatory Attribution** | The Fan Creator must give explicit, visible credit to the Original IP Holder. |
| üö´ **Exploitation Ban** | Content may not be used in projects involving **unauthorized NFTs** or other predatory practices. |
| üõ°Ô∏è **Statement of Tolerance** | Includes an ethical clause requesting the Original IP Holder to avoid arbitrary takedowns and provide advance notice. |
| ‚õî **Strict Ban on Sale and Microtransactions** | Fan Content is **strictly non-commercial** and **MAY NOT BE SOLD** as a product. All revenue models like microtransactions, paid DLCs, loot boxes, or subscriptions are prohibited. **The only exception is pure, voluntary, non-essential donations.** |

### ‚õî Exception: The Only Path to Sale Monetization

The Fan Creator **MAY ONLY** transition to monetization by sale if:
1.  **They Remove All Derivative Content:** The creator must remove *all* assets, lore, names, and visual elements that are not original to the fan, making the work a fully original product. **OR**
2.  **They Obtain Explicit Permission:** They receive specific written authorization from the Original IP Holder.
**If either condition is met, the work is no longer governed by the GameFair License.**

## üß≠ The 4 GameFair Variations (Choose Your Sharing Level)

| License | Full Name | Primary Purpose | What it Changes in Sharing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. GF-V1-SA** | **Share-Alike** | **Promote open growth and collaboration.** | **Requires reciprocity:** Anyone who modifies your work must also license their new work under GF-V1-SA. |
| **2. GF-V1-ND** | **No Derivatives** | **Protect artistic integrity.** | **Prohibits modification:** Content can only be copied and shared in its complete, original form. |
| **3. GF-V1-AU** | **Alternate Universe** | **Recognize and protect another fan's AU.** | **Requires Dual Attribution** (Original IP + AU Creator) and mandates reciprocity within the AU framework. |
| **4. GF-V1-CC** | **Community Contribution** | **Assign authorship to a collective.** | **Defines Collective Ownership:** Authorship is assigned to the "Project/Community," ensuring contributions are an irrevocable common good. |

**The full legal text for each variation is available in the respective `LICENSE_[XX].txt` files in this repository.**
